Crystal Collector

In this game you'll be clicking on the four crystals below to reach the magical number. When you reach the correct number you win! Try to see if you can get it!


Each crystal will have a seperate value assigned to it. Try to guess the right combination in order to make them equal up to the same value of your goal.


When you hit the number or bust and go over, the game will reset so you can play again.
